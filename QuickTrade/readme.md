    <article class="markdown-body entry-content p-3 p-md-6" itemprop="text"><h2><a id="user-content-important" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#important"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>IMPORTANT</h2>
<p>This addon requires the addon "TradeNPC" to be installed and loaded before use.
TradeNPC can be found here: <a href="https://github.com/Ivaar/Windower-addons">https://github.com/Ivaar/Windower-addons</a></p>
<p>The addon Itemizer is required if you would like to use the command '//qtr all' to also trade items that are in your Mog Sack or Mog Case.
Itemizer can be downloaded from the Windower launcher or found here: <a href="http://docs.windower.net/addons/itemizer/" rel="nofollow">http://docs.windower.net/addons/itemizer/</a></p>
<h2><a id="user-content-what-does-this-addon-do" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#what-does-this-addon-do"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>What does this addon do?</h2>
<p>Example Video: <a href="https://youtu.be/TVFWjjP_Dh0" rel="nofollow">https://youtu.be/TVFWjjP_Dh0</a></p>
<p>QuickTrade searches your inventory for items that are tradeable or storable with certain NPCs, then sends a command to be used by TradeNPC.
This allows you to automatically trade multiple stacks of items repeatedly with a command instead of using the much slower trade menu.
QuickTrade will inform you of how many times you need to enter //qtr in order to complete all available trades.
For up to 10 seconds after the trade is complete, it will skip any conversations with the NPC.</p>
<p>Valid trades are as follow:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Elemental Crystals and Clusters: Trade with Ephemeral Moogles for storage or with Waypoints to accumulate Kinetic Units.</p>

<h2><a id="user-content-example" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#example"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Example</h2>
<p>You have 3 stacks of Fire Crystals, 1 stack of Ice Crystals, and 2 Fire Clusters in your inventory that you would like to store.
Travel to and target one of the Ephemeral Moogles or Waypoints you would like to trade to.
In the chat window, enter '//qtr' without the quotes and press enter, or enter 'qtr' in the console.
The addon will cause TradeNPC to instantly trade all 3 stacks of Fire Crystals and the 2 Fire Clusters with one command.
After waiting a few seconds, target the moogle or Waypoint again and re-enter the command.
The addon will then cause TradeNPC to instantly trade the stack of Ice Crystals.
If you run the command again, you will be informed that you have no more items to trade.</p>
<p>The behavior is similiar when trading other valid items with valid NPCs.</p>
<p>You will need to enter the command once for each crystal element or item type you possess in your inventory.
This seems to be a game limitation since most NPCs accept only one type of item at a time.</p>
<p>As of 2018.09.07, a new argument 'loop' was added. This will allow QuickTrade to perform all possible trades with the NPC.
For example, if you have 10 Special Gobbie Keys, visit a gobbie and enter '//qtr loop' and it will trade all 10 keys for you,
one after the other, until you run out of keys or inventory space. Entering '//qtr loop 5' will allow QuickTrade to loop a maximum of 5 times,
instead of using all 10 keys.</p>
<p>If you have the Itemizer addon and would like to also trade any valid items that you may have in your Mog Case or Mog Sack,
enter the command '//qtr all'. This will allow QuickTrade to search your other valid storage areas and use Itemizer to move the items
into your inventory automatically.</p>
<p>If you wish to test this addon before having it perform a live trade, you may edit line 36 in the QuickTrade.lua file.</p>
<p>Change
exampleOnly = false
to
exampleOnly = true</p>
<p>This will cause the addon to print the command to be issued in the windower console.
When you are ready to allow the addon to trade for you, change the 'true' back to 'false'.</p>
<h2><a id="user-content-installation" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#installation"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Installation</h2>
<p>Browse to your Windower\addons folder and create a new folder inside called "QuickTrade"
Place QuickTrade.lua in this new folder.
Load the addon by accessing the console from within FFXI and typing 'lua l QuickTrade'</p>
<p>If you want this addon to be loaded automatically every time you launch the game,
add 'lua l QuickTrade' to the bottom of the file "Windower\scripts\init.txt"</p>
<p>I would also recommend having the addons TradeNPC and Itemizer load automatically by adding
'lua l TradeNPC' and 'lua l itemizer' to your init.txt.</p>
<p>If TradeNPC is not loaded when using this addon, the trade command will be issued but nothing will happen.
The same is true regarding Itemizer. Commands will be issued, but nothing will happen.</p>
